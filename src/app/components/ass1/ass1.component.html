The Organization

The purpose of this assignment is to familiarize yourself with your organization (internal/external-macro/micro) environment, to go out there and speak to the right people to get to know your organization, and then give your own opinion on how you feel things should work, what is working and what is not.

  • Describe the role IT plays within your organization.
  • Illustrate the SWOT analysis of your company/department/unit

Project Management

The purpose of this assignment is to demonstrate what you've learnt and what project management experiences and knowledge you practiced towards the projects assigned to you.

  • Identify the scope of your industry exposure period.
  • Document a project plan (including activities and deliverables) of your industry exposure period
